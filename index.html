Malik Wensman's Autobiography

Malik Wensman is a student at NC State University in Raleigh, NC. He is a Freshman, but has enough credits making him qualify as a Sophmore in MyPack. He will graduate in 2026. He chose the major of Mechanical Engineering because he believes it will allow him to build great machines in his future.


Malik Wensman is currenly working as a tutor at Mathnasium. He helps students from all grade levels improve in math. This job is a big step up from his last one where he had to make pizzas all day and not be allowed to take any free food home. His dream job is to be an entreprenuer with a business that creates machines that will impact peoples lives in a positive way.
